Timeless Ceramic Tiles — Handpicked from Morbi

At VAISHNO MARBLE & GRANITE we curate an extensive collection of ceramic tiles in varied sizes, finishes and textures. Authentic Sourcing

All tiles are sourced directly from Morbi, Gujarat — a region world-renowned for ceramic and vitrified tile manufacturing. By importing directly we ensure consistent quality, finish and fast availability for large projects.

Wide Size Range

We stock a broad range of sizes including 4×2, 2×2, 12×18, 12×12 and many more. Whether it's a compact bathroom or a vast commercial floor, we have tile dimensions that fit your space perfectly.

Custom Printed Tiles

Create a signature look with custom printed tiles. Bring your design — a logo, pattern or photo — and we'll convert it into durable ceramic prints that remain vibrant for years.
